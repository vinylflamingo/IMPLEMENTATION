PERMIT RENEWAL UTILITY

A utility designed to solve a time issue.

In my support role I came across a issue. A large amount of data needed to be reviewed within a short deadline. The data normaly is entered in through a large enterprise software, with many features and less than ideal performance. I developed a utility to minimize the amount of information to just what needs to be reveiwed and made changes to the SQL Server database as needed. The minification of UI and Data Access allowed me to complete a single data review in an average of 2s where using the enterprise software the average was closer to 7s.
